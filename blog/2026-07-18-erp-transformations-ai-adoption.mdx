AI adoption on ERP data is an **ERP transformation problem** wearing a model badge. If item master, scan timing, and costing layers are unreliable, the model will learn confident wrong answers.

{/* truncate */}

## Three foundations

Before predictive or agentic layers:

1. **Warehouse truth** — scan-at-event discipline, location master, exception reason codes ([Supply Chain and Warehouse](/docs/erp-transformation/supply-chain-and-warehouse))
2. **Costing integrity** — FIFO layer rules and reconciliation ([FIFO and Inventory Costing](/docs/erp-transformation/fifo-and-inventory-costing))
3. **Data quality gates** — ownership matrix and evidence bundles ([ERP Data Quality](/docs/erp-transformation/erp-data-quality))

## Readiness before models

Run the [AI Readiness Diagnostic](https://github.com/Brilliant-Brainstorm-Intelligence-LLC/bbi-ai-readiness-diagnostic-kit) on the specific operational decision you want to improve. If data readiness or evidence quality scores below critical floors, fix the ledger before tuning hyperparameters.
