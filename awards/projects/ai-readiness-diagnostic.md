## Problem

Organizations often start AI work with a model or vendor before clarifying decisions, data, authority, failure modes, and evidence.

## Method

Evidence-first diagnostic scoring across business value, data readiness, human oversight, governance, delivery readiness, and evidence quality—with explicit human-authority boundaries.
