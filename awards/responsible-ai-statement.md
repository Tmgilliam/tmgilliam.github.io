# Responsible AI statement (public-safe)

Brilliant Brainstorm Intelligence treats AI as a consequential decision system, not a novelty feature.

## Principles

1. **Evidence before implementation** — clarify decisions, data, authority, and failure modes before model or vendor selection.
2. **Human authority** — consequential actions require clear ownership, escalation, and approval boundaries.
3. **Transparent methods** — scoring, assumptions, and limitations are documented for independent inspection.
4. **Privacy by design** — public proof never requires exposing proprietary or confidential operating records.
5. **Honest maturity** — planned work is labeled planned; private systems are labeled private.

## Product application

The BBI AI Readiness Diagnostic Kit operationalizes readiness across business value, data readiness, human oversight, governance, delivery readiness, and evidence quality. It is designed to surface gaps—not to certify “AI ready” without evidence.

## Submission rule

Award entries must not present planned interactive demos, private BBIOS capabilities, or unverified outcomes as completed public products.
