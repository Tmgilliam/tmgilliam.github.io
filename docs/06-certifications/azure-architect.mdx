## What AZ-305 validates

The Solutions Architect Expert exam assesses ability to design solutions across Azure pillars:

- **Identity and governance** — Entra ID, RBAC, policy, landing zones
- **Compute and applications** — App Service, containers, functions, integration patterns
- **Data platforms** — SQL, Cosmos DB, storage, analytics architecture choices
- **Networking** — Segmentation, hybrid connectivity, private endpoints
- **Business continuity** — Backup, DR, RTO/RPO tradeoffs
- **Migration and modernization** — Assessment, phased move, cutover risk

AZ-305 is design-focused—it complements AZ-104's operational administration depth.
