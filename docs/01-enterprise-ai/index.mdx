# Enterprise AI

Enterprise AI succeeds when it improves a real decision, respects human authority, and can be verified without hype. This section documents the public methods and tools that reflect that operating model.

## Operating principles

### Evidence before implementation

Organizations often commit to a model, vendor, or agent framework before they have clarified the business decision, required data, accountable owner, failure consequences, or evidence needed to justify the investment. The diagnostic kit reverses that sequence.

### Human authority where consequences matter

AI can analyze, organize, recommend, and execute **authorized** actions. Consequential decisions remain explicitly human unless a bounded, reviewed control says otherwise.

### Public proof without private exposure

Public repositories provide reusable tools, sanitized patterns, and technical examples. They do not expose confidential implementation details, private operating records, client information, or employer-confidential material.
